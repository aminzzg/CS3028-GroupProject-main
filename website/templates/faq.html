Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using the Staff Workload Management System.

Getting Started

How do I log in?

Use your university-provided login details. If login fails, contact your system administrator.

Who can use this system?

Admins, Managers, and Staff members all have access with different permissions.

Timetables & Hours

How do I view my timetable?

Click “MyTimetable” on the left sidebar. Admins/Managers can also view staff timetables.

What does "Target Hours" mean?

Target hours represent how many hours a staff member is expected to work per week.

Notifications & Messages

Who can send messages?

Staff and Managers can send messages to one another. Admins do not use the messaging system.

How do I mark a message as read?

Open a message and click “Mark as Read”.

Technical

I forgot my password.

Your password is your first name and your last name with the first letter of each capitalized.

Which browser should I use?

Google Chrome is recommended.
